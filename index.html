rules

  • The game has 2 players, playing in rounds
  • In each turn, a player rolls both dice as many times as player wishes ,Each result get added to the ROUND score
  • BUT.... If player roll dices and get 1 in any one dice, all ROUND scores gets lost.
  • Now, its second player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'hold' ,which means ROUND score get added to GLOBAL score , then it's next player's turn
  • The first player to reach GOAL points on GLOBAL score wins the game.
